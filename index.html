Our Research Join Us

Welcome to PCGL
in Chungnam National University!

Our lab aims to build crop genomic big data through a multidisciplinary approach including genomics, phenomics, and bioinformatics, and to develop AI-based predictive breeding technology. Through this, we aim to secure the sustainability of future food production and contribute to human health and well-being.

Members of the PCGL study bioinformatics, plant genomics, and plant genetics. We always welcome those who are interested and passionate in these fields.

Research Areas

Plant Genomics

Genome assembly, annotation, comparative genomics and evolutionary study of crop species.

AI-based Digital Breeding

Machine learning and deep learning models for genomic selection and predictive breeding.

Big Data & Bioinformatics

Large-scale genomic database construction and computational pipeline development.

Phenomics

High-throughput plant phenotyping integrated with multi-omics data.
